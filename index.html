Insights

Short, practical takes you can reuse in governed environments.

Why scoring fails without categorisation

Risk work, exit work, strategic enablement, and optimisation behave differently. Force like-for-like comparison first, then score within category.

Principle: Not all work should compete with all other work.

Clarity is an outcome, not a survey

If clarity exists, it’s visible in decision speed, fewer escalations, and better quality inputs — not self-reported maturity.

Principle: Evidence beats advocacy.
